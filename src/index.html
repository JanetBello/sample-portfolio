About me

Hello! My name is Yusting (Yuxin to friends) and I have a great affinity for collaborative work, I like to create designs that inspire and involve people. As a UI designer, I believe that a good product not only provides a solution but also creates an emotional experience for the people who use it. In my creative process I use tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop and Illustrator that help me in the workflow for the production of user interfaces.
